Significance funnel: number of walk-forward cells surviving each test stage, by model. Stages are raw directional accuracy above chance, the binomial sign test, Clark--West, Diebold--Mariano vs.\ the random walk, and the Bonferroni / Benjamini--Hochberg multiple-testing thresholds (all at $\alpha=q=0.05$). HTBoost ($t$) has $N=145$ family cells; the others $150$. The collapse from raw to corrected counts is the chapter's null-confirmation result.
